BottleMe Privacy Policy / BottleMe 隐私政策

Last updated: 2026-05-26 / 最后更新日期：2026-05-26

1. App Description / 应用说明

BottleMe（中文名：寄给海）是一款匿名漂流瓶社交应用。用户可以发布文字瓶子、接收来自其他用户的瓶子、进行匿名回复、查看回声消息，并使用 AI 辅助润色表达、生成回复建议、识别内容主题与情绪，以帮助用户更自然地表达和交流。

BottleMe is an anonymous drift bottle social app. Users can post text bottles, receive bottles from other users, reply anonymously, view echoes, and use AI-assisted features such as text polishing, reply suggestions, topic and emotion detection to help users express themselves more naturally.

本应用强调匿名社交体验。除非用户主动提供，否则本应用不会要求用户公开真实姓名、手机号、邮箱、身份证件信息等个人身份信息。

BottleMe is designed around anonymous social interaction. Unless users voluntarily provide such information, the app does not require users to publicly disclose real names, phone numbers, email addresses, identity documents, or similar personal identity information.

为了提供应用功能、保障社区安全并优化产品体验，本应用可能收集和处理以下信息：

To provide app functionality, maintain community safety, and improve the user experience, BottleMe may collect and process the following information:

  • 账号与登录信息：例如 Firebase 匿名账号标识符、Google 登录相关标识符、应用实例 ID 等。
    Account and sign-in information, such as Firebase anonymous user identifiers, Google sign-in identifiers, app instance IDs, and similar identifiers.
  • 用户生成内容：例如用户发布的瓶子内容、回复内容、聊天消息、心情标签、主题标签、收藏、举报、屏蔽等操作记录。
    User-generated content, such as bottle text, replies, chat messages, mood tags, topic tags, saved items, reports, blocks, and related activity records.
  • 应用使用数据：例如页面访问、功能点击、会话信息、崩溃日志、性能数据等。
    App usage data, such as screen views, feature interactions, session information, crash logs, and performance data.
  • 设备与技术信息：例如设备型号、系统版本、语言设置、应用版本、网络状态、IP 地址、请求时间等。
    Device and technical information, such as device model, operating system version, language settings, app version, network status, IP address, request time, and similar technical data.
  • 推送通知信息：例如用于发送通知的设备推送令牌。
    Push notification information, such as device push tokens used to send notifications.

上述信息主要用于：

We use this information mainly for the following purposes:

  • 提供投递瓶子、捞取瓶子、匿名回复、消息通知等核心功能。
    To provide core features such as casting bottles, catching bottles, anonymous replies, and notifications.
  • 进行内容安全审核、举报处理、反垃圾信息、防滥用和账号安全保护。
    To perform content safety review, handle reports, prevent spam and abuse, and protect account security.
  • 提供 AI 润色、AI 回复建议、主题/情绪识别、相似内容匹配等功能。
    To provide AI polishing, AI reply suggestions, topic/emotion detection, and similar-content matching.
  • 分析应用使用情况，优化产品体验、稳定性和服务质量。
    To analyze app usage and improve product experience, stability, and service quality.
  • 遵守法律法规、平台政策或监管要求。
    To comply with applicable laws, platform policies, and regulatory requirements.

3. Anonymous Social Features and User Content / 匿名社交与用户内容

BottleMe 默认以匿名方式展示用户内容。其他用户通常无法看到您的真实身份信息。

BottleMe displays user content anonymously by default. Other users generally cannot see your real identity information.

但请注意，匿名并不意味着内容完全不可追溯。为了保障社区安全、处理举报、防止骚扰、欺诈、垃圾信息或其他违规行为，本应用可能会在服务器端保存与内容相关的账号标识、时间、状态、举报记录及必要的技术信息。

However, anonymity does not mean content is completely untraceable. To protect community safety, handle reports, and prevent harassment, fraud, spam, or other violations, BottleMe may store account identifiers, timestamps, status records, report records, and necessary technical information related to user content on our servers.

请不要在瓶子、回复或聊天内容中主动发布敏感个人信息，例如真实姓名、住址、手机号、邮箱、身份证件信息、银行卡信息、精确位置、密码或其他隐私信息。

Please do not voluntarily post sensitive personal information in bottles, replies, or chats, such as real names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, identity document information, bank card information, precise location, passwords, or other private information.

4. AI Features and Data Processing / AI 功能与数据处理

本应用可能提供 AI 辅助功能，包括但不限于：

BottleMe may provide AI-assisted features, including but not limited to:

  • 润色用户输入的瓶子内容。
    Polishing bottle text entered by users.
  • 生成匿名回复或破冰建议。
    Generating anonymous replies or icebreaker suggestions.
  • 识别内容的情绪、主题和风险类型。
    Detecting the emotion, topic, and risk category of content.
  • 辅助进行内容安全审核和推荐匹配。
    Assisting with content safety review and recommendation matching.

当用户使用 AI 功能时，用户输入的文本、相关上下文、标签或必要的技术信息可能会被发送至本应用的服务器及第三方 AI 服务提供方进行处理。AI 生成结果可能不完全准确，用户应自行判断是否采用。

When users use AI features, the text they enter, related context, tags, or necessary technical information may be sent to BottleMe servers and third-party AI service providers for processing. AI-generated results may be inaccurate or incomplete, and users should decide whether to use them.

本应用不会故意要求用户向 AI 功能提交敏感个人信息。请避免在使用 AI 功能时输入不必要的个人隐私或敏感信息。

BottleMe does not intentionally require users to submit sensitive personal information to AI features. Please avoid entering unnecessary private or sensitive information when using AI features.

本应用可能会在用户设备本地保存部分数据，例如登录状态、草稿、偏好设置、缓存内容、最近使用记录等，用于提升加载速度和使用体验。用户可以通过系统设置清除应用数据，清除后部分功能可能需要重新登录或重新加载。

BottleMe may store certain data locally on the user's device, such as sign-in status, drafts, preferences, cached content, and recent usage records, to improve loading speed and user experience. Users can clear app data through system settings. After clearing data, some features may require signing in again or reloading content.
