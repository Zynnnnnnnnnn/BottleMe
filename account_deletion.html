BottleMe Account and Data Deletion

BottleMe 账号与数据删除

BottleMe supports account and data deletion requests.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your BottleMe account and related data, please email us at:

dannyhcnice@gmail.com

Please include the following information:

  • Your BottleMe user ID, if available in the app settings page
  • The Google account email used to sign in, if you linked Sign in with Google
  • A short request such as: “Please delete my BottleMe account and data.”

Data we delete

  • Account identifier and profile data
  • Bottles you created
  • Replies and chat messages associated with your account
  • Saved items and app preferences

Data we may retain

We may retain limited records where necessary for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or enforcement of our community rules. These records may include reports, moderation logs, safety logs, and transaction or technical records where applicable.

Processing time

We will process deletion requests within a reasonable period, usually within 30 days, unless a longer retention period is required by law or for legitimate safety reasons.
